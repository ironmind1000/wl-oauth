Privacy Policy — Whispered Legends

Last updated: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Whispered Legends Poster" application ("the App") handles information. The App is a private tool operated by the owner of the Whispered Legends channel ("we", "us") for the sole purpose of publishing our own short videos to our own social media accounts via official platform APIs (TikTok, Meta/Instagram, YouTube).

Information we access

  • Account authorization tokens. When you (the account owner) authorize the App, the platform issues an access token that lets the App upload videos to that account. We access only the scopes required to publish content (e.g. basic account info and video upload/publish).
  • Our own content. The video files, titles, descriptions and hashtags that we create and choose to publish.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, access, or store personal data of any third-party users.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share any data with third parties.
  • We do not use the App for advertising, profiling, or analytics on other people.

How information is used

Access tokens are used exclusively to publish our own videos to our own connected accounts. Tokens are stored privately on the operator's own computer and are never published, shared, or transmitted to anyone other than the official platform APIs.

Data retention

Access tokens are kept only as long as needed to operate the App and can be revoked at any time by the account owner from the platform's app/security settings, which immediately ends the App's access.

Security

Credentials are stored locally with restricted access and are excluded from any source control.
